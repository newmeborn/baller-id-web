Delete Your Baller.ID Account

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

Send an email to our support address with your request:

  1. Email noreply@ballerid.in from the address linked to your account
  2. Subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your in-app display name so we can locate your account
Send Deletion Request

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, jersey number, avatar)
  • Your match history and stats
  • Your votes and ratings
  • Your authentication credentials (email + password)
  • Your push notification token

Retention period

Deletion is processed within 30 days of your request. Aggregated, anonymised match statistics (e.g. team scores) may be retained for app integrity but cannot be linked back to you.

Note: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Your player card, points, and ranking will be removed from all leaderboards.
