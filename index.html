HOW IT WORKS

Earn it on the pitch.

The card grows with you, match by match. Six steps from signup to elite.

01

Create your account.

Sign up with email. Name yourself. Pick your position. 30 seconds and your card exists.

02

Set the match.

Pick a date, pick a turf. Drop the link in the group chat.

03

Squad up.

Your squad taps in, picks a side, locks it down. Add them to your locker room while you're at it.

04

Run the match.

Real pitch, real legs. Play your game — we'll be here when you're done.

05

Drop the stats.

Goals, assists, saves. Your team confirms it. Fakes get flagged. Truth wins.

06

Watch yourself level.

Bronze → Silver → Gold → Elite. Every honest match shapes the card. You earn every step.

THE THIRD COLUMN

ATK and DEF you score.
REP you earn.

Anyone can have a good match. Few can be the player everyone trusts to log it straight, week after week. That's what REP measures — and it's the column that can't be padded.

🤝

Show up. Repeatedly.

Consistency over heroics. The ballers who turn up every weekend build it faster than the ones who score five and ghost.

✍️

Log your stats. Honestly.

Submit what actually happened — not what you wish happened. Your teammates see every number before it counts.

✓

Get confirmed. Stay clean.

Every match your team verifies adds to it. Get flagged for inflating numbers and it drops just as fast.
